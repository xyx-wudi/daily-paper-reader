## 摘要
策略学习主导着多智能体系统中集体合作的演化。尽管演化结果强烈依赖于策略学习过程中智能体可获得的信息，但大多数研究将信息的来源和数量视为随时间固定不变。然而在现实中，个体不断接触到随时间变化的外部信息。本文提出了一个关于带有时间信息的演化动力学的通用框架，该框架使用时序网络来刻画策略学习可用信息的动态变化，时变连接决定了每个智能体的信息状态。在合成网络和实证网络中，我们发现与静态网络相比，时间信息持续促进合作。我们进一步证明，随着时序网络变得稀疏，这种优势变得更加显著。这种模式的出现是因为稀疏化放大了智能体之间的信息异质性，造成了学习信息获取的不平等，这有助于合作策略的传播，这与静态信息中异质性通常抑制合作形成了鲜明对比。受此启发，我们开发了一种可解释的算法，通过生成具有可调异质性的学习网络，大幅增强了不同网络中的合作。我们的结果将时间信息识别为一种现实且广泛适用的促进集体合作的机制。

## Abstract
Strategy learning governs the evolution of collective cooperation in multi-agent systems. Although evolutionary outcomes depend strongly on the information available to agents during strategy learning, most studies treat both the source and amount of that information as fixed over time. In reality, however, individuals are continually exposed to external information that varies over time. Here we develop a general framework for evolutionary dynamics with temporal information, which uses temporal networks to characterize dynamic changes in information available for strategy learning, with time-varying connections determining each agent's information state. Across synthetic and empirical networks, we find that temporal information consistently promotes cooperation relative to their static counterparts. We further demonstrate that this advantage becomes more pronounced as temporal networks become sparser. This pattern arises because sparsification amplifies information heterogeneity among agents, creating unequal access to learning information that can facilitate the spread of cooperative strategies, in sharp contrast to static information formulations where heterogeneity often suppresses cooperation. Guided by this insight, we develop an interpretable algorithm that substantially enhances cooperation across diverse networks by generating learning networks with tunable heterogeneity. Our results identify temporal information as a realistic and broadly applicable mechanism for promoting collective cooperation.
